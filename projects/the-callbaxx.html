The Callbaxx

This is a website developed for use by the Cornell Callbaxx A Capella group. It is designed to showcase their music, performances, members, alumni, and give general information such as upcoming events and audition information. It has editing functionality so that the members will be able to update information and maintain the site for future use. The site was built with HTML, CSS, Javascript, and PHP, and is currently deployed through the use of Heroku, PostgreSQL, and AWS S3.
