Pikachu Dash

This simple Android mobile game features everyone's favorite yellow Pok√©mon running and jumping over rolling boulders. The player's goal is to have Pikachu jump over as many boulders as possible before getting hit and ending the game. The only gameplay mechanic is that Pikachu jumps upon user clicking. The boulders are randomized in speed and size, but they will tend to get bigger and faster as the user gets further into their run. This project was developed in Android Studio.
