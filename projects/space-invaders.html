Space Invaders

Space Invaders is a variation of the original, classic Space Invaders arcade game. It was made for an assignment in my CS 1110 class, so much of the game had already been given to us by the professor. The themes of the game are pretty similar with the original one it's based off of. Aliens move across the screen and then downwards, and the player ship must eradicate all aliens before they reach the bottom line, or before all lives are lost. However, variations upon the original include the addition of powerups, and a final boss: Professor Walker White, alienized!
