CoursePlan
Best Cornell Hack

Cornell CoursePlan is a web application developed by me and 2 friends to aid Cornell students in their future course-planning. Development of the web application began at BigRed//Hacks Spring 2018, where it won Best Cornell Hack. Following the hackathon the project was revisited to add functionality before being released for public use. The site is available for use only to the Cornell community, and it allows students to create multiple plans for future courseloads and also keep track of the requirements they need to fill for their major and college. It is developed with HTML, CSS, Javascript, and PHP, and is supported by a MySql database that allows students to save their plan data to their account.
